What is Krishnamurti Paddhati (KP)?

Krishnamurti Paddhati is a system of astrology developed by Prof. K.S. Krishnamurti that combines traditional Indian astrology with aspects of Western astrology. It emphasizes the stellar (nakshatra) positions and sub-lords to provide more precise predictions.

How is KP Astrology different from traditional Vedic astrology?

KP Astrology differs by using the Placidus house system instead of the equal house division used in Vedic astrology. It focuses more on nakshatras and sub-lords, offering higher accuracy in predicting timing of events like marriage, job, or childbirth.

What are Sub-Lords in KP Astrology?

In KP Astrology, each nakshatra is divided into 9 unequal segments called sub-lords. The sub-lord plays a crucial role in fine-tuning the prediction and timing of events.
